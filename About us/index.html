What We Are

Legally Structured Fund for Investor Protection

The Company operates as a Segregated Cell Company under the provisions of The Segregated Account Companies Act 2004 & The International Business Companies Act, 2000, as amended. Incorporated on August 12, 2022, under registration number 177594 B, the Company follows a robust legal framework that enhances asset security and regulatory compliance.

What We Do

Diversified Investment Strategy for Enhanced Returns and Long-Term Growth

The Company employs a diversified investment strategy by allocating capital to high-performing third-party funds and offering private credit to select legal entities. This approach enhances portfolio returns, manages risk, and ensures consistent income and long-term growth. With a focus on due diligence and strong partnerships, the Company supports the success of the businesses it finances while optimizing returns for its investors.

A Unified Purpose: Our Vision & Mission

At JBB Investment Fund, our vision is to lead with innovation and integrity, while our mission is to provide personalized investment solutions that drive sustainable financial growth and success

our vision

To be a trusted leader in sustainable investment, driving long-term economic growth and value creation for our investors. We aim to achieve this by building a resilient portfolio that prioritizes responsible investment practices, innovative strategies, and diversification across asset classes and markets.
